Facilities

Library

Our college boasts a well-stocked and modern library, providing students with a vast collection of academic resources and reading materials. Whether you seek textbooks, research papers, or recreational literature, our library is the perfect sanctuary for knowledge seekers. Students have access to the latest research publications and study materials, fostering a conducive environment for research and academic growth.

Laboratories

The college is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories for various subjects, including physics, chemistry, biology, and computer science. Students can conduct experiments and practical work under the guidance of experienced faculty. Our cutting-edge laboratories are equipped with advanced technology and equipment, providing students with hands-on experience and opportunities to conduct groundbreaking experiments across various disciplines. Under the guidance of skilled faculty, students can delve into practical applications of theoretical concepts, honing their analytical and problem-solving skills.

Sports Facilities

Dedicated to promoting physical fitness and well-being, our college boasts extensive sports facilities, including outdoor sports fields, indoor courts, and a fully-equipped gym. Students can engage in a wide array of sports and recreational activities, nurturing a healthy lifestyle and team spirit within the college community.

Hostel

The college hostel ensures a comfortable and secure living space for outstation students, creating a close-knit community that supports personal and academic development. With 24/7 security and dedicated wardens, students can focus on their studies while forging lifelong friendships and memorable experiences.

College Canteen

Our vibrant canteen is a popular hub on campus, offering a diverse menu of nutritious and delicious meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices. Designed to cater to various culinary preferences, the canteen provides students with a convenient space to relax, recharge, and socialize during their breaks.

Conference Halls

Our state-of-the-art conference hall is an ideal venue for hosting academic conferences, seminars, and workshops. With modern audio-visual facilities, high-speed internet, and ample seating capacity, the hall ensures seamless presentations and fosters intellectual discussions among scholars and participants.
