Courses

"Choose from the courses mentioned below"

BBA

BBA (Master of Business Administration) is an undergraduate degree program spanning three years, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of business concepts, core subjects like management, marketing, finance, and optional specializations for career readiness in diverse business roles.

MBA

MBA (Master of Business Administration) is a postgraduate degree program typically lasting two years, focusing on advanced business management education and leadership skills for professionals seeking career advancement and managerial positions.

MBBS

MBBS is an undergraduate medical degree that prepares students to become qualified doctors and surgeons.

MD

MD (Doctor of Medicine) is a postgraduate medical degree that allows physicians to specialize in a specific field of medicine after completing their MBBS.

Btech

BTech (Bachelor of Technology) is an undergraduate engineering degree program that focuses on the practical application of engineering principles.

Mtech

MTech (Master of Technology) is a postgraduate engineering degree that provides specialized knowledge and advanced skills in a specific area of technology.

B.Arch

BArch is an undergraduate degree program that trains students in the art and science of architectural design, preparing them to become qualified architects.

BDS

BDS is an undergraduate dental degree program that trains students to become licensed dentists, focusing on dental anatomy, oral health, and dental treatment procedures.

MDS

MDS is a postgraduate dental degree program that allows dentists to specialize in a specific field of dentistry, such as orthodontics, oral surgery, or prosthodontics.
